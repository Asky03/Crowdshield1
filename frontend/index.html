Analysis Tips

📸 Images — JPG or PNG of a crowd. Works best with overhead or angled shots where people are visible.


🎬 Videos — MP4 up to 100MB. The system samples every 15th frame and also detects crowd movement using optical flow.


🌡️ Heatmap — Generated automatically when ML service is running. Shows hotspot zones overlaid on the original image.


📍 Location Tag — Add a location so you can filter results by zone later.
